Hello everyone, my name is Mary Christine Sacay. My nickname is MC. I was born on the 18th of September 1996. I am a 26-year-old passionate individual who finds joy in various creative pursuits. Cooking, singing, and writing music are my outlets for self-expression, allowing me to channel my emotions and share my thoughts with others. I am constantly experimenting in the kitchen, trying new recipes and flavors to create delicious and wholesome meals. When I'm not in the kitchen, you'll often find me belting out tunes or composing my own songs, as music is a language that speaks to my soul. Another aspect of my adventurous spirit is my love for biking. Exploring new places on my bike brings me a sense of freedom and allows me to appreciate the beauty of the world around me. Oh, and did I mention I'm a proud cat person? Their independent yet affectionate nature resonates with me.

Aside from my creative pursuits, I also have a deep appreciation for knowledge. Reading novels allows me to escape into different worlds and perspectives, while watching documentaries satisfies my curiosity about the world we live in. These activities provide me with a wealth of inspiration and broaden my understanding of the human experience.
